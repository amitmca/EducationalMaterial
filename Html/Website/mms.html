Marathi Medium School



Marathi Medium School is the pioneer to the rural children of the area. The school, which started in 1992 with 125 children has turned out to be Almamater for 1500 children today. The school runs classes up to the std. X'th and follows CBSE syllabus. The school is divided into three standards. They are Pri-Primary, primary & Secondary. Each division is run by a separate set of Staff and teachers, headed by separate Headmistress. The school not only maintains the standard specified by the CBSE for the students and teachers; but also follows the direction of the Government regarding fees and other educational matters. A palatial building with spacious rooms and a large play ground offer opportunity to the students to pursue their studies and extra-curricular activities. Regular participation in co-curricular and extra-curricular activities, compulsory computer training and NCC training are the instruments for personality development of the students. Vidya prathisthan's M.M. School provides two well-equipped separate laboratories for performing science practical. Separate library containing reading hall is also developed. The school possesses ' House System" for all-round development. And the scholarship examination of std. VIII conducted nationwide each year, the school has kept its steady mark of getting at least two students in the selection list. 93.26 % percent result in the SSC examination for class - X'th in the year 2001-2002 has proved the dedication and the determination of Mrs. G.M. Kshrisagar, the principal of the school, and her staff to develop the school as one of the best in Maharashtra. Now Semi-English pattern has been started from 2002 for desiring students.

Vision and Mission

Vidya pratishtan, founded in the year 1972 by the visionary, Hon. Shri. Sharad Pawar with an English Medium School. The trust now houses an Arts, Science and Commerce college along with the three schools, a B.Ed college , Law College and Microbiology College. It has also started with Engineering college at VIIT in the academic year 2000-2001.

On the 12th February 2000, the Vidya Pratishtan’s Institute Of Information Technology Was inaugurated at the auspicious hands of Hon.Shri.Nara Chandra Babu Naidu, Chief Minister Of Andhra Pradesh. From then to date it has been a memorable experience for one and all who hold this institute dear to their hearts.

Since it’s inception, VIIT was honored to receive the staunch support of CDAC. Today, we are proud to present our forth consecutive graduates of CDAC’s Diploma In Advanced Computing or DAC as it is fondly known. Also, From the Academic Year 2001-2002 VIIT has started M.C.A Course Which is a Post Graduate Course Under PUNE UNIVERSITY.

VIIT Regularly offers the Diploma In Advanced Computing, every 6 months. Learning being a continuous process for all, We at VIIT are dedicated and keen to create an effective Learning environment for one and all. VIIT believes in the "Gurukul" system of education in which the student stays on campus and is given his teachings in complete dedication. This not only enhances the quality of from the student but also adds value and a new meaning to the teacher-student relationship. Wherien the teachers not only act as guides but also as mentors and friends to the students. Thus enabling the teacher to bring out the best in the student.

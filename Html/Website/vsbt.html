Our Mission

“Education is the training, which sharpens the intellect, improves grasping power
and develops the faculty of discrimination among students.”

Vidya Pratishthan has always been dedicated to this ideal.

We feel the need to prepare our students to meet the challenges that globalization poses to them
in the new competitive atmosphere in the country and in fact, worldwide. Though our students are
being prepared for the highly competitive global marketplace, we feel that an essential part of
our educational policy is to imbide a socio-economic sensitivity to their perspective.

Our aim is to aid rural development through quality education.

Our focus is holistic, on character building through a well rounded range of activities
including the curricular, extra-curricular and co-curricular.

Welcome To VIIT

Vidya Pratishthan's Institute of Information Technology (VIIT) was established in February 2000 at Baramati with an aim to provide quality education in the field of Information Technology and Computer Science. In these three years this institute is proved to be one of the best IT institute conducting various Post Graduate, Graduate, Diploma and Autonomous courses related to IT and Computer Science.

VIIT offers courses like MCA and MCM affiliated to University of Pune, BCA & MBA courses are affiliated to Tilak Maharashtra Vidyapeeth

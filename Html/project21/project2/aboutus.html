About Us

	
	
     Vidya Pratishthan's Institute of Information Technology (VIIT) was
established in February 2000 at Baramati with an aim to provide quality 
education in the field of Information Technology and Computer Science. 
In these three years this institute isproved to be one of the best IT 
institute conducting various Post Graduate, Graduate,Diploma and 
Autonomous courses related to IT and Computer Science. 
     VIIT offers courses like MCA,MBA and MCM affiliated to University of 
Pune, BCA & MBA courses are affiliated to Tilak Maharashtra Vidyapeeth and
VIIT also offers courses like B.Tech(Electronics), Diploma in Electronics, 
Diploma in Computer Technology,Diploma in Instrumentation Engineering which 
are affilated to Yashwantrao Cahvan Maharashtra Open University, Nashik.VIIT
is spreading on a campus of 30 acres,this institute provides best available 
infrastructure and amenities for its students.VIIT has a network of more that 
250 computers powered with Gigabit Fiber Optic Cable.Two boys hostels and one 
girls hostel enjoys the connectivity to main servers of VIIT.These hostels
have a capacity to accommodate around 1000 students.Computerized library,
huge auditorium, workshop, various labs for engineering activities are 
the unique rudiments of VIIT.

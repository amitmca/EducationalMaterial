'Vande Matharam' another retune
by Indiaglitz.com
 Chennai, June 28 2006

After making waves with his patriotic album Vande Matharam produced in collaboration with Bharat Bala Productions, music director A R Rahman is all set to repeat the same.

Rahman has retuned the national anthem which is soulful and very catchy.

On Monday, Jana Gana Mana retuned by Rahman was premiered at a grand function in Mumbai where laurels were heaped on Rahman for his astounding work. The song will be screened before films in multiplexes.

Rahman, singer Kavita Krishnamurthy among others took part in the function.

Speaking on the occasion, the guests expressed the unanimous view that the national anthem tuned by Rahman will appeal to the young and old alike, instilling a fresh sense of patriotism.

With the voices of Lata Mangeshkar, Pandit Jasraj, Pandit Bhimsen Joshi, Asha Bhosle, Dr. Balamuralikrishna, Bhupen Hazarika, Jagjit Singh, Hariharan, Kavita Krishnamurthy and Sadiq Khan Langa, this rendition of the national anthem is sure to create a powerful impact among audiences.

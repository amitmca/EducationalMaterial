Rules and Regulations :

      1)  Fees are taken by Cash or by Demand-draft or by cheque only.

        2)  One student get only one room.

        3)  They have to take care their goods themselves.

        4)  Students have to take admissions within the given duration in the hostel.

        5) Parents can meet the students twice in a month.

         6)  Students do not allow smoking and drinking  in the hostel.

         7)  After 10 p.m., students are not allowed to go outside the hostel

         8)  Boys are not allowed in the girls hostel and Girls are not allowed in the boys hostel.

         9)  Hostel is made for the students and they will take care of everything about the students complaints.

        10) Students have to take admission with original documents and xerox copies of certificates.

        11) One room should be allocated for 2 Students of same course only.

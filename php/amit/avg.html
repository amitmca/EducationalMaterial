AVERAGE COSTING METHOD

FORMULA :---
Average Cost =Total Cost of goods available for sale / Total units available

EXAMPLE :---

Date
Transaction
No.Of Items
Unit Cost
Total Cost
June 1 Begining Inventory 50 1.00 50.00
June 6 Purchased 50 1.10 55.00
June 11 Purchased 150 1.20 180.00
June 20 Purchased 100 1.30 130.00
June 25 Purchased 150 1.40 210.00
Total 500 625.00

Total Cost of goods available for sale = 625
Total units available = 500
Average Cost = 625/500 = 1.25
June 30 Ending Inventory 220 1.25 275.00

Cost of goods available for sale = 625
less(-) June 30 Inventory = 275
Cost of goods sold = 350

FIRST IN FIRST OUT(FIFO)

CONCEPT :---
1) Materials which are received First are,issued first.

2) After first lot is over the next lot is taken for supply.

EXAMPLE :---
April 1 200 units are purchased @ Rs 5 per unit

April 3 500 units are purchased @ Rs 6 per unit

April 5 500 units are issued to job no 1001

The issue of 300 units will be priced as under.......

From the first lot : 200 units @ Rs 5 = 1000 Rs
Remaining 100 units from second lot : 100 units @ Rs 6 = 600 Rs
Total material issued = 1600 Rs
Closing Stock : 2400 Rs

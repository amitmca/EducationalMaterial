LAST IN FIRST OUT(LIFO)

CONCEPT :---
1) Materials which are received Last are,issued first.

EXAMPLE :---
April 1 500 units are purchased @ Rs 5 per unit

April 3 300 units are purchased @ Rs 6 per unit

April 6 400 units are issued to job no 1001

The issue of 400 units will be priced as under.......

From the first lot : 300 units @ Rs 6 = 1800 Rs
Remaining 100 units from second lot : 100 units @ Rs 5 = 500 Rs
Total material issued = 2300 Rs
Closing Stock : 2000 Rs
